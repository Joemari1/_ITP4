About Passi

Passi City, located in the province of Iloilo, Philippines, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and warm hospitality. It is famously called the "Sweet City" due to its thriving sugarcane industry and the production of muscovado sugar.

The city is also home to the world-renowned Patadyong, a traditional handwoven fabric that showcases the creativity and skill of local weavers. Passi City offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity, making it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking an authentic Filipino experience.

From its lush landscapes and historical landmarks to its colorful festivals and delicious cuisine, Passi City promises an unforgettable journey for every visitor.

Top Attractions

Passi City Plaza

A beautiful park in the heart of the city, perfect for relaxation and community events.

Patadyong Weaving Center

Experience the traditional weaving of the famous Patadyong fabric.

San Juan Bautista Parish Church

A historic church known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.

Lake Passi

A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for picnics and nature walks.

Passi City Museum

Discover the rich history and culture of Passi City through artifacts and exhibits.

Passi Public Market

A bustling market where you can find fresh produce, local delicacies, and handmade crafts.

Bulanon Falls

A stunning waterfall nestled in the mountains, ideal for hiking and swimming.

Sugarcane Plantations

Explore the vast sugarcane fields that contribute to the city's sweet reputation.

Passi Eco-Park

A nature park with walking trails, picnic areas, and a variety of flora and fauna.

Our Lady of Lourdes Shrine

A peaceful religious site with a beautiful statue of the Virgin Mary.
